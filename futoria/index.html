Futoria Home Solutions Pvt. Ltd is a leading manufacturer of uPVC windows and doors in Kerala, known for its visually appealing and high-quality products. Established in 2015, Futoria uPVC Windows & Doors emerged from a collaboration with LG Korea, the world's largest manufacturers of electronics, housing systems, and automobile components.

We offer a wide range of products, including casement windows and doors, tilt and turn windows, sliding windows and doors, designer windows, conservatory, and lift and slide doors, all manufactured with precision and attention to detail. Our products are compliant with the latest quality standards and reinforced with galvanized steel for added safety and security.

We prioritize environmental preservation and conduct all business activities in a sustainable manner.
